What is the minimum drainage easement for a 24 inch RCP pipe?
A 24-inch pipe falls under "Diameter equal to or less than 30 inches." The minimum easement is:
"Easement shall be 10 feet either side of the centerline of the pipe."
For cover ≥ 5 feet, minimum easement width is 20 feet.
